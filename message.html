Hong Kong

I grew up in Hong Kong, a densely populated city located at the south of China. The food in Hong Kong is delicious and I promised myself if I ever go back to Hong Kong, the first thing I will do is to buy a lot of food and eat.

kindergarten

In my memory, the earliest thing I could remember is at kindergarten. My memory about this part was quite blurry, so I mainly just remember that I got really angry, and I tried flip over a table inside the classroom. As expected, the teachers tried to stop me.

One interesting memory is that I was walking in the corridoor, and for some reason I bump into the wall and hit my nose. As I feel the pain, I saw blood comes out my nose. I wasn't freaked out or anything, but it was my first time having a nose bleed (And last, I have never had another nose bleed after that.), so I kinda got excited about it and rush into the bathroom to get tissue only to get told off not to run.

Do I trust myself's memory? To be honest, No. But I do think that it went the way it is.
